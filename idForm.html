Identification Card Request

This form is required when requesting the City of San Antonio Identification Card (CoSA ID), CoSA Facility access or Facility access change. If the request if for access to facilities requiring a Criminal Justice Information System (CJIS) Clearance, a background check will be required prior to the issuance of an ID card. By submitting this form, all individuals listed indicate the person obtaining a CoSA ID has fulfilled all requirements to include the background checks as outlined within the CJIS Security Policy and CoSA CJIS Guidelines (as required)

The information contained in this form will be maintained in accordance with all applicable privacy laws
